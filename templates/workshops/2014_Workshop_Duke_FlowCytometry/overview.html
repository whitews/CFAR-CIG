Workshop Overview

The Duke CFAR Flow Cytometry Workshop is an intensive week-long wet-workshop that includes lectures focusing on qualifying and standardizing instruments and reagents.

No previous flow cytometry experience is required; however, the benefits of attending the Workshop will be much greater with at least some basic working knowledge about flow cytometry.

The goal of the workshop is two-fold:

  1. Participants will be able to go home and perform the advanced 11-color ICS assay illustrated during the Workshop
  2. Participants will be able to apply the basic principles reviewed during the Workshop to modify the 11-color ICS assay to suite their laboratory needs.
